Dakka La Range Recipe

[Image of Dakka La Range - golden lentil curry garnished with coriander]

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, mincedðŸ¦†
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly until water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, combine lentils, water, turmeric, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until lentils are soft and mushy. Stir occasionally and add water if needed.
  4. In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil over medium heat.
  5. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  6. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. SautÃ© for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  8. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and mushyðŸ¦†.
  9. Add red chili powder and garam masala. Mix well.
  10. Pour this tempering over the cooked lentils and mix thoroughly.
  11. Simmer for 5 more minutes to blend the flavors.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  13. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Cooking Time: 35-40 minutes
Serves: 4-6 people
